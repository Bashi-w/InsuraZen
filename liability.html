What is Liability Insurance?

Liability insurance serves as your financial shield in the event of an unfortunate accident on the road. It's a specialized coverage designed to protect you from the financial consequences of causing injury to others or damaging their property while driving.

Bodily Injury Liability


Imagine a scenario where an accident leads to injuries to other individuals. Bodily Injury Liability coverage steps in to cover medical expenses, rehabilitation costs, lost wages, and even legal fees if you're found responsible for the accident.

Property Damage Liability


Accidents can also result in damage to other people's property, including vehicles, fences, or structures. Property Damage Liability coverage ensures that the costs of repairing or replacing the damaged property are taken care of, easing your financial burden.

Eligibility Criteria

Driver's License: All applicants must possess a valid and active driver's license recognized by the local authorities in Sri Lanka.

Vehicle Registration: The vehicle being insured should be registered and in compliance with Sri Lankan regulations.

Usage: The intended use of the vehicle should be specified, whether it's for personal use, commercial use, or both.

Driving History: Applicants' driving records will be assessed. A history of accidents or traffic violations might affect eligibility.

Insurance History: Previous insurance coverage and claim history could impact eligibility and premium calculations.
