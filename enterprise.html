What is the Insurazen Enterprise Retirement Insurance Plan?

At Insurazen, we understand that securing your financial future is a priority, especially during your retirement years. That's why we are pleased to introduce our Enterprise Retirement Insurance Plan—a comprehensive and tailor-made solution designed to offer financial security and peace of mind to both individuals and businesses.

Our Enterprise Plan is a versatile and robust retirement insurance offering that goes beyond the ordinary. It's uniquely crafted to meet the specific needs of businesses, employers, and employees, ensuring that everyone involved can enjoy the benefits of a secure retirement.

Eligibility Criteria


For Employers:

Business Type: Employers from various business types, including small and large enterprises, corporations, non-profits, and other organizations, are eligible to enroll in the Enterprise Retirement Insurance Plan.

Legal Status: Employers must have a valid legal entity status in the region where the plan is offered and comply with all relevant regulatory requirements.

Financial Capacity: Employers must demonstrate the financial capacity to make contributions to the retirement plan for their employees.

Compliance with Regulatory Requirements: Employers must adhere to all local and national regulations related to employee benefits, retirement plans, and insurance offerings.



For Employees:

Employment Status: Employees must be currently employed by a participating employer offering the Enterprise Retirement Insurance Plan.

Consent and Participation: Employees must voluntarily choose to participate in the Enterprise Plan and provide consent for their contributions to be deducted from their salaries or compensation.

Legal Capacity: Employees must have the legal capacity to enter into a financial contract. Minors and individuals lacking legal capacity may require a legal guardian to participate on their behalf.

Beneficiary Designation: Employees are encouraged to designate beneficiaries to ensure the efficient distribution of retirement benefits in the event of their passing.

Tax Considerations: Employees should understand and comply with any tax implications related to their contributions and benefits under the Enterprise Retirement Insurance Plan.
