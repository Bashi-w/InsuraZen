InsuraZen Life Legacy Plan


What is the InsuraZen Protection Insurance Life Legacy Plan?

At Insurazen, we understand that planning for the unexpected is a crucial aspect of life. Our Life Legacy Plan, a key offering under our comprehensive protection insurance solutions, is designed to provide you and your loved ones with the peace of mind that comes from knowing that their financial future is safeguarded, no matter what life brings your way.

The Life Legacy Plan is more than just insurance; it's a commitment to protecting the ones you hold dear. This plan is thoughtfully designed to offer financial security, support, and a lasting legacy for your loved ones in times of need.

Features

Financial Protection in the event of death

Customizable Coverage

Legacy Planning for beneficaries

Flexible Premiums

Tax-Efficient Benefits

Eligibility Criteria

Age Requirements: Applicants must fall within the age range, 18 to 65 years old, to be eligible for the Life Legacy Plan.

Residency: Applicants must be residents or citizens of the region where the plan is offered. Proof of residency or citizenship may be required.

Legal Capacity: Applicants must have the legal capacity to enter into a contractual agreement. Minors or individuals lacking legal capacity may require a legal guardian to apply on their behalf.

Consent and Disclosure: Applicants should provide accurate and complete information on the application form and agree to the terms and conditions outlined in the policy contract.

Financial Capacity: Applicants must demonstrate their ability to pay the required premiums for the Life Legacy Plan. Premium payments can be made at varying intervals, such as monthly, quarterly, or annually.
